530 Type Club

530 is a font-collective that hosts workshops and studies on experimental typography.

Clara Chirila-Rus is a graphic designer based in Los Angeles focusing on editorial design, printed matter, and handmade web development.

Gilles Goosen is based in Amsterdam with a passion for printed matter, typography, and visual identities.

Jonas Riemersma is a graphic designer, typography enthusiast, and prolific experimenter based in Arnhem.

Joshua van Blijderveen is a graphic designer and artist based in the Netherlands with a curiosity in typography, photography, and printed matter.

Soyoon Cha is a graphic designer in based in South Korea.

Previous members of 530 include Ben McMillan (2019), Dong Bin Han (2019), Joost Vullings (2019), Helena Hesselink (2023), Kuan-Ting Chen (2023), Kasper Quaink (2023).
